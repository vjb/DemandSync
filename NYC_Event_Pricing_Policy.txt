DEMANDSYNC CORPORATE PRICING POLICY - NEW YORK CITY EVENTS

1. SEVERE WEATHER: During extreme weather alerts (Blizzard, Flash Flood, Hurricane, Extreme Heatwave), markup on essential weather-related items (generators, neck fans, shovels, umbrellas) is strictly capped at +15% over base MSRP to prevent price gouging.

2. SPORTS & ENTERTAINMENT: During major sporting events (US Open Tennis, NYC Marathon), markup on non-essential spectator apparel and gear can be increased by up to +35% depending on supply and demand. 

3. GENERAL ANOMALIES: All other spontaneous demand anomalies are capped at +20%.

Any calculated price that violates these constraints must be overridden and clamped to the maximum allowable threshold.
